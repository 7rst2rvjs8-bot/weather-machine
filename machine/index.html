The machine

An instrument first.

Most of the work so far has gone into proving the harness — the execution, logging, and test scaffolding — is trustworthy, kept strictly apart from whether the edge exists. A clean instrument can still measure an edge that isn't there. Conflating the two is the failure the project is built to avoid.

01  /  Two separate questions

What's certified is the instrument — not the edge.

[ Certified ]

The harness

Are the measurements real — clean fills, honest logs, no leakage between what's modeled and what's recorded? This is what the gate certifies.

[ Unconfirmed ]

The edge

Does any strategy actually make money? Stated as open. A trustworthy instrument is the precondition for asking — never the answer.

02  /  The architecture

A book of strategies, mostly still ahead.

META-LAYER
Allocates capital across strategies and prices in selection bias over the count of strategies it chooses among. The destination, not today's reality.
STRATEGY #1
The champion — a momentum/breakout approach on short-dated options. Works in backtest; has never traded live. The crude first model, by design.
SHADOW STREAMS
Run live during market hours, logging every signal that would have fired — accumulating forward evidence before a dollar is risked.
UNIVERSE
9 liquid names — broad-market ETFs plus a few high-volume single names. Specific tickers and signal mechanics are deliberately not published.
