What is this, really?

This is one person’s attempt to build a system that trades the stock market a little more wisely over time — not by being a genius once, but by being honest about its mistakes. Every time it guesses wrong, it writes that down and fixes one error, the way weather forecasts slowly got more accurate over decades. Right now it is very early: the system has passed a safety check, but it has not yet been shown to actually make money — and this site is careful never to pretend otherwise.

That’s the whole thing. Everything else is detail.

A few honest notes before you explore. Investing money you can’t afford to lose is risky, and options — the kind of trades this system makes — are riskier than most. Nothing here is advice. There are no profit claims anywhere on this site, because there are no profits yet, by design. What you’re looking at is the careful, unglamorous groundwork that has to come before anyone can honestly say whether an idea works.

If you remember one thing

“The machine works reliably” and “the machine makes money” are two different claims. This project has earned the first and is being very careful about the second. Telling them apart is the entire discipline.
