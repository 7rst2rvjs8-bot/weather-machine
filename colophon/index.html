A note on privacy

What’s shown, what’s private — and why.

This site has two jobs: to show the work honestly, and to stay understandable to someone with no finance background. Neither job requires giving away the parts that have to stay private in a real, live system.

What’s shown — all of it on purpose. The method and the rule it lives by. The findings so far, including the unflattering ones. The plain, coarse status of where things stand. The shape of what’s being traded — how many names, what kind. And the full road ahead. If something would help you understand the approach or judge it fairly, it’s here.

What’s kept private — also on purpose. A few things stay out: account and broker details, the exact positions held at any moment, the specific tickers being traded, and the precise logic of the signals. These are withheld to protect a working edge if one turns out to exist — not to inflate one. There’s an important difference, and this project tries hard to stay on the right side of it.

The honest version

Nothing here claims a profit, because there isn’t one yet, by design. What’s hidden is hidden for the ordinary reason any real trader keeps an edge to themselves — not to hide a hole in the story.
