The method

An error-correction engine.

Forecasting became reliable by recording where each model was wrong and adding the one term that would have caught the miss. The same architecture runs here — a book of strategies, each correcting itself forward.

01 / The engine

The champion is the crude first model.

The aim is not one clever model. It is a book of independent strategies, each running its own error-correction loop. Strategy #1 is the deliberate baseline. Every later addition is a correction term — admitted because it catches a specific, recurring class of miss, not because it improves a backtest.

The governing rule

A correction term earns its place only by catching a named miss in data the system has never seen.

02 / The loop

One cycle, turned over and over.

01 · TRADE

Take the position

Acted on live, or logged as a signal that would have fired.

02 · LOG THE MISS

Record where it was wrong

The miss is the fuel. Nothing improves without an honest record of the failure.

03 · PROPOSE THE TERM

One correction

The single term that would have caught that named miss — a fresh hypothesis, not a tweak.

04 · FORWARD-TEST

On unseen days

Shadowed forward against data it has never touched. Never rescued by re-reading the past.

05 · PROMOTE / KILL

Earned, or retired

Cheap to kill, hard to promote. A failure retires it; survival is always provisional.

// A miss with no name is not a correction term and does not enter the roadmap. The loop turns no faster than market days accrue.

03 / The search rule

Wide over the unknown. Narrow at the evidenced gate.

Two activities stay strictly apart, because collapsing them is how a system fools itself.

Characterization · wide, free

Learning

Every candidate enters as data. Its properties are logged per signal, never gated on. A sound signal whose costs eat it is a finding, not a silent rejection.

Selection · narrow, deflated

Promotion

Funding a strategy is a separate, pre-committed gate under full multiple-comparisons deflation. The wide net informs that gate without being it.

The load-bearing line

The wide net feeds learning, never selection. Characterization is free; comparison spends statistical power; the two stay on separate layers.

04 / The cost of the past

History is a budget that only spends down.

Each test against the fixed historical window spends one comparison from a finite budget — roughly twenty are already gone. Mine it hard enough and any system looks certain in backtest and behaves like noise live. The target is complex and forward-validated, one term at a time; the discipline exists to protect the loop, not to keep things simple.
