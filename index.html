KATABASIS

Katabasis is built on a single rule: assume every trading idea is noise until it proves otherwise — and almost nothing does. The point of the system is not to find winners. It is to keep itself honest about what it does not yet know.

What it is

A small live instrument, and a large research factory.

One part is a protected live instrument that watches a slice of the market in real time and never acts without evidence. The other is a research factory that studies far more history than any live system could — turning millions of market observations into labelled outcomes, candidate ideas, and validators whose job is to refuse to believe a result until it survives on data it has never seen.

01 · WATCH

Listen

The live instrument watches the market and records what it sees.

02 · DIAGNOSE

Study

The research factory looks for patterns, and just as hard for reasons a pattern might be a mirage.

03 · PROVE

Believe slowly

Nothing is trusted until it holds up forward, on days no one had seen when the idea was formed.

The first instrument

It began as the Weather Machine.

Before Katabasis was a research factory, it was one instrument: a live shadow watching a small slice of the options market, waiting for pressure changes, calibrating itself, and refusing to act without evidence. That instrument taught the project its first discipline — do not trust a signal just because it appears. It still runs, protected and unchanged, as one chamber inside the larger system.
