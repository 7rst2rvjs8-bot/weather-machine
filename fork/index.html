The fork

Three roads, held open.

The first live trade reaches a genuine decision point. The three directions below are not ranked and not pre-judged — each waits for a rested decision once the evidence is in. Naming them now commits the choice to evidence, not momentum.

The decision · after the first trade
CHANGE COURSE
Turn toward slower, structural kinds of strategy — a different shape of advantage with different failure modes, run through the same prove-it-forward process from scratch.
GROW IT
Keep today's approach and trade it at larger size — along a pre-set schedule that scales on evidence, never on a date or a hunch.
STEP BACK
Re-examine whether a real advantage clears the bar at all — and be willing to conclude the honest answer is “not yet,” or “not here.”

// The road where the project might stop is on the map on purpose. The other two are honest only beside it.
