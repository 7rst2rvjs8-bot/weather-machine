The fork

Three roads, held open.

After the first real trade, the project reaches a genuine decision point. The three directions below are not ranked and not pre-judged — they wait for a rested decision once the evidence is actually in. Naming them now is a promise to choose on evidence, not on momentum.

The decision · after the first trade
CHANGE COURSE
Turn toward slower, steadier kinds of strategies — a different shape of advantage with different risks, run through the same prove-it-forward process from scratch.
GROW IT
Keep today’s approach and trade it at larger size — but only along a pre-set schedule that scales up on evidence, never on a date or a hunch.
STEP BACK
Re-examine whether there’s any real advantage here at all — and be willing to conclude the honest answer is “not yet,” or “not here.”
