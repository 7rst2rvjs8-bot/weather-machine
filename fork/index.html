The fork

Three roads, held open.

After the first real fill, the project reaches a genuine decision point. The directions below are not ranked and not pre-judged — they're deferred to a rested decision once the evidence is actually in. Naming them now is a commitment to choose on evidence, not on momentum.

The decision · post-fill
PIVOT
Turn toward slower, volatility-structural strategies — a different shape of edge with different failure modes, run through the same forward-validation loop from scratch.
SCALE
Keep the current approach and grow it at larger capital — only along the pre-registered, evidence-gated sizing schedule, never by calendar or conviction.
STEP BACK
Re-examine whether any edge clears the bar at all — and be willing to conclude that the honest answer is "not yet," or "not here."

// The fork is on the map on purpose: a project that can't name the road where it might stop isn't being honest about the other two.
